Nerve Pain: What's Really Behind It?

Simply put, nerve damage does not just happen because of "aging" or "poor circulation." Breakthrough research reveals it occurs when three specific enzymes—COX-2, PGE-2, and MMP-13—become overactive. These toxic enzymes aggressively strip away the collagen "glue" that protects and binds your sensory nerve endings.

When these nerve endings are exposed and degraded, the central nervous system goes into overdrive. This triggers the classic symptoms: tingling, burning sensations, numbness in hands and feet, and sharp, shooting electrical pain. Left unchecked, this constant overfiring leads to muscle fatigue, anxiety, and long-term nerve degeneration.

To combat this persistent discomfort and restore nerve health, you must suppress these three overactive enzymes at the root. Specialized "inside-out" nerve formulas effectively target this process, allowing the nerve pathways to finally rest, repair, and regenerate.

The remarkable result: Proper enzyme-targeting supplementation can significantly contribute to deep neural healing and lasting neuropathy restoration, without the dangerous side effects of prescription medications.
